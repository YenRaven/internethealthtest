Welcome to the Internet Health Test!

The Health Test runs a few short speed tests to some measurement servers located near you to compare how your broadband connection varies to different parts of the Internet. These tests shouldn't take too long and do not collect personal information from your browser.


To learn more about the data collected see the Privacy Policy.
