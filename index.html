Welcome to the Internet Health Test!

The Health Test runs a few short speed tests to some measurement servers located near you to compare how your broadband connection varies to different parts of the Internet. These tests shouldn't take too long and do not collect personal information from your browser.

ISP’s have degraded the performance of connections between networks on the Internet in order to charge tolls. The result is that access to content such as Netflix slows down for everyone until the ISPs are paid. This test runs speed measurements to different locations across the Internet to find points of congestion that could be degrading your access. Running this test provides data, and gives you some information about your connection performance from your ISP to a number of key locations.
